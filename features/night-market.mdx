---
title: "Night Market"
description: "Discounted skin offers during Night Market events"
---

Night Market is a limited-time Valorant event where Riot gives every player a personally curated set of six weapon skins at a significant discount. Your offers are unique to your account — no two players see the same Night Market.

## What Valstore shows during Night Market

When Night Market is active, the Night Market tab displays all six of your discounted offers. Each skin card shows:

- **Skin name and tier icon** — full skin name with its content tier badge
- **Original VP cost** — the regular price shown with a strikethrough
- **Discounted VP cost** — the reduced price highlighted in red
- **Percentage savings** — the discount percentage displayed next to the price (e.g. `-49%`)
- **Countdown timer** — a live days/hours/minutes countdown in the app header showing when Night Market ends

Tapping a skin card opens the skin detail page with video previews of its levels and chromas.

## When Night Market is inactive

Night Market only runs during specific periods each act. When no Night Market event is running, Valstore detects this because the storefront API response does not include a `BonusStore` field. In that case, the Night Market tab shows a message indicating that no Night Market is currently available.

<Note>
  Because Night Market offers are personalized by Riot, the skins you see will differ from every other player's, even within the same account region.
</Note>

## Background notification

<Info>
  Valstore schedules a background task called `NightMarketRenewal` via WorkManager. When this task runs and detects that a Night Market event has become active, it sends a push notification — "Night market is back!" — so you know to check your offers without opening the app.
</Info>
