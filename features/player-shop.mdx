title: "Player shop"
description: "Your four daily rotating skin offers"
---

Every Valorant account receives four weapon skin offers that rotate every 24 hours. The player shop screen lets you check those offers at any time without opening the game.

## What you see on the shop screen

Each skin card shows:

- **Skin name** — the full display name of the skin
- **Tier icon** — the content tier badge displayed in the top-right corner of the card
- **Tier color** — the card background gradient uses the tier's signature color so you can identify rarity at a glance
- **VP price** — the Valorant Points cost shown in the bottom-right alongside the VP currency icon
- **Countdown timer** — a live countdown in the app header showing how much time remains before the shop refreshes

Tapping any skin card opens the skin detail screen, where you can watch its unlock video (levels and chromas) if preview footage is available from the Riot API.

## Content tiers

Valstore color-codes each offer by its content tier based on the skin's VP price:

| Tier | VP cost | Card color |
|------|---------|------------|
| Select | 875 VP | Blue (`#5b9cdd`) |
| Deluxe | 1,275 VP | Teal (`#28bda7`) |
| Premium | 1,775 VP | Pink (`#cb558d`) |
| Exclusive | 2,175 VP | Orange (`#fd9257`) |
| Ultra | 2,475 VP | Gold (`#eed878`) |

The tier icon is sourced from the Riot content tier asset endpoint and rendered as a watermark behind the skin image.

## Shop refresh mechanism

Valstore registers a periodic background task called `ValStoreStoreRenewal` using WorkManager. This task runs silently even when the app is closed, re-authenticates your session, fetches the new daily offers, and fires a push notification if any wishlisted skin appears in the rotation.

<Tip>
  Add skins to your wishlist to receive a push notification the moment they appear in your daily shop — no need to check the app manually.
</Tip>
