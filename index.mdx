## What is Valstore?

Valstore is an open-source Flutter app that connects to Riot's in-game APIs to display your Valorant store on your mobile device — no need to launch the full game just to check your offers.

<Note>
  Valstore is an unofficial third-party app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Riot Games. The project has been archived by the original author; the Google Play listing was removed at Riot's request. You can still build and sideload the app from this repository.
</Note>

## How it works

Valstore authenticates you via Riot's OAuth flow inside a secure in-app WebView. Your access token is stored locally and used to fetch storefront data directly from Riot's private API endpoints. No credentials are ever sent to Valstore's own servers — the only third-party backend used is Firebase for the community skin database and wishlist sync.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the APK">
    Download the latest release APK from the [GitHub releases page](https://github.com/ruzbyte/valstore-mobile/releases) and sideload it onto your Android device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your region">
    Choose your Valorant region (EU, NA, AP, KR, BR, LATAM) on the region selection screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with Riot">
    An in-app browser opens the official Riot authentication page. Enter your Riot credentials — Valstore never sees your password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your shop">
    Your daily offers load automatically. Browse the shop, Night Market, and bundles in real time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
